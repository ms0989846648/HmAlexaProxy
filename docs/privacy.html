Privacy Notice for HmAlexaProxy

HmAlexaProxy is a local‐network application that uses Login with Amazon to fetch your Alexa Home Graph data (including Ring camera streams). We do not store, share, or sell any personal data beyond what’s required to retrieve and display your device information.

Data Collected: Temporary OAuth tokens to call the Alexa Home Graph API; device metadata returned by that API.

Data Retention: No data is persisted to disk—everything is fetched on demand and held in memory only.
